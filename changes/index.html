Meet the cars

Dora - Fiat Panda

With a top speed of 78 mph and a 0 to 60 of 22.3 seconds, lets hope this little fiat panda makes it to mongolia in one piece!

The Rat - Toyota Yaris

With a 62 BHP 1.0 l engine, 13.2 second 0-60 and a 96 mph top speed, this little rocket has been Matt’s choice of transport for over 4 years, can it make it to Mongolia?

Freddie - Peugeot 205

With a Goliath 43HP, 0-60 in under a minute (same as a Bugatti Veyron), and no registered top speed (because it’s that high), there couldn’t be a better car to drive to Mongolia.

... AND THEY'RE OFF!

This summer Matt, Charlie, Chris, Richard, Benny, Ed and George will be undertaking the Mongol Rally - An epic 10,000 miles journey spanning the breadth of Europe and Asia unsupported and unaided starting in London and ending in Ulan Baataar - Mongolia.

ALL FOR CHARITY

To mark the momentous event we are hoping to raise a target of £4000 for a selection of kick-arse charities: Cool Earth, Cancer Research UK, The Honeypot Children's Charity and Brain Tumour Research.
